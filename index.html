Product Description — 5-in-1 Tactical EMS Pants

Overview: Built for field durability and quick access, these pants keep critical tools stable while staying comfortable on long shifts and cramped transports.

  • Reinforced seat/knees; articulated fit for crouching/lifts.
  • Zoned stretch panels prevent seam stress.
  • Tool pockets: shears slot, radio/phone pocket, dual penlight sleeves, glove stash.
  • Moisture-wicking waistband with silicone grip.
  • Bar-tacked stress points; YKK zips; low-noise hardware.
  • Low-placed reflective accents for visibility without glare.

Technical specs: Fabric 65% Poly / 35% Cotton ripstop (DWR); weight ~650 g (M); athletic fit with gusseted crotch; colors Night Navy, Asphalt, Ranger Green; sizes 28–44 regular/long; care: machine wash cold, tumble low.

Why it helps: Less drag, faster draws, fewer seam failures during kneels, lifts, and rigging.
